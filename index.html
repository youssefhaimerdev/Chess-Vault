▸ FAQ

QUESTIONS?

Is ChessVault really free? +
Yes, completely free. No subscription, no premium tier, no paywall. We support the site through non-intrusive ads. Every feature is available to everyone.
Do I need to create an account? +
No login required. Your progress, scores, and Chess Report are saved automatically in your browser's local storage. Just close the tab and it's there next time.
What level is ChessVault for? +
Beginners through intermediate players (roughly 600–1800 Elo) will benefit most. The opening lines, explanations, and training modes are designed to build real understanding, not just rote memorization.
How is this different from other chess trainers? +
ChessVault combines multiple training modes that reinforce each other: drilling the lines, learning the ideas behind moves, testing under pressure with Chaos Mode, and identifying your weak spots with the Chess Report. Most trainers do one of these well. We do all of them.
Will you add more openings? +
Yes! The opening library is modular — we add new packs regularly. Coming soon: Ruy Lopez, King's Indian, Queen's Gambit Declined, and more. The architecture is designed to grow.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes, fully responsive. The trainer works on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. Drag and drop or tap to move pieces. The layout adapts to your screen size.
